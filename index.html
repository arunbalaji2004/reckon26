About the Institution

PSG College of Arts & Science, Coimbatore, is one of Tamil Nadu's most prestigious autonomous institutions. Established in 1947 under the aegis of the PSG & Sons' Charities Trust, the college has stood as a beacon of academic excellence for over seven decades.

Accredited with the highest NAAC A++ grade, the institution offers more than 60 undergraduate, postgraduate and research programmes spanning arts, science, commerce and management disciplines. The college is known for its vibrant campus life, highly qualified faculty, and state-of-the-art infrastructure.

With a legacy of producing scholars, leaders and innovators, PSG Arts & Science continues to shape the intellectual landscape of Tamil Nadu and beyond — nurturing both analytical rigour and creative thinking in every student.

THE ORGANIZERS

Department of Mathematics (SF)

The Department of Mathematics (Self-Finance) at PSG College of Arts & Science has been a crucible of analytical thinking and quantitative excellence. Our faculty and students consistently push the boundaries of mathematical inquiry — from pure theory to real-world applications in data science, cryptography, and computational modeling.


In association with the Institution's Innovation Council (IIC), we proudly present RECKON'26 — a celebration of intellect, creativity, and competitive spirit that brings together the brightest minds from colleges across the region.

CO-ORGANIZER

Institution's Innovation Council

The Institution's Innovation Council (IIC) is a Government of India initiative under the Ministry of Education's Innovation Cell, aimed at creating a vibrant local innovation ecosystem in higher education institutions. IIC at PSG College of Arts & Science fosters a culture of innovation, entrepreneurship, and research among students and faculty alike.


Through its association with the Department of Mathematics (SF), the IIC co-presents RECKON'26 — an event designed to challenge boundaries, ignite curiosity, and celebrate the unique fusion of mathematics, strategy, creativity, and intellect.
